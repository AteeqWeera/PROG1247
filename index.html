Rumi (Jalauddin Balkhi): The Mystic Poet

biography

"Rumi was born in Balkh, Afghanistan in 1207. He was a persian poet,
Islamic scholar, theologian and Sufi mystic.
He is best known for his poetry which has been translated into many languages.
His works are widely read in Iran, Afghanistan, Tajikistan,
Turkey and the United States. He is considered one of the greatest poets"

Rumi was born in Balkh, in present-day Afghanistan,
and later moved to Konya, in present-day Turkey, where he spent most of his life.

Famous Quote

One of Rumi's famous quotes: "The minute I heard my first love story, I started looking for you,
not knowing how blind that was. Lovers don't finally meet somewhere. They're in each other all along."
